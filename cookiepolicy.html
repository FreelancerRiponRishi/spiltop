Cookie Policy for SpilTop

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how SpilTop ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website, www.spiltop.dk (the "Website"). It describes what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the website. They help us remember your preferences, analyse your site usage, and tailor content to your interests.

Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To improve navigation and user experience on our Website.

  • To collect statistical data on how our Website is used, allowing us to improve our content and services.

  • To display content and offers tailored to your preferences.

  • To ensure the proper functioning of certain essential features on our Website.

What Types of Cookies Do We Use?

We use different types of cookies to meet the specific needs of our site:

  • Essential Cookies (Nødvendige Cookies): These cookies are indispensable to the functioning of the site, allowing you to navigate between pages and access certain features. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, security, and accessing secure areas. These cookies cannot be deactivated in our systems as they are necessary for the website to work.

  • Performance Cookies (Ydelses Cookies): These cookies collect anonymous data concerning the use of the site, such as which pages are visited most often and how users navigate the site, in order to improve its performance and efficiency. This data helps us understand how visitors interact with our Website so we can continually optimize it.

  • Personalization Cookies (Funktionalitets Cookies): These cookies allow us to personalize the content according to your preferences, such as remembering your language choice or region, thus offering you a more adapted and enhanced experience. They remember choices you make to improve your features.

  • Marketing/Advertising Cookies (Marketing/Annoncerings Cookies): These are used to display targeted advertisements based on your interests and to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. They may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites.

How to Manage Cookies?

You can adjust your preferences regarding cookies at any time by modifying your browser settings. You have the option to accept or refuse the use of cookies. Note that some features of the site may be limited or not function correctly if certain cookies are deactivated. Consult the "Help" section of your browser to learn how to adjust cookie settings.
For specific opt-outs for third-party services (like analytics or advertising networks), please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Updates to this Cookie Policy

This policy may be updated periodically in order to reflect changes in the use of cookies or to comply with legal requirements. We invite you to consult this page regularly to take note of the most recent version of our cookie policy. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
